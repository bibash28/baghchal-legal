2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Usage Data: App interactions, screen views, button taps, game events (e.g. game started, game completed, level selected). Collected via Microsoft Clarity.
  • Crash Reports: Automatic crash logs and error reports collected via Firebase Crashlytics to help us fix bugs.

We do not collect your name, precise location, payment information, or any sensitive personal data.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

5. Data Retention

Crash reports and analytics data are retained according to the respective third-party service policies (typically 90 days for Crashlytics, 13 months for Clarity). Feedback emails are retained in our inbox until manually deleted.
